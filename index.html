About Vince Kumar

Vince Kumar has been in the television industry for 25 years. He has made several successful American and Indian television episodes. He recently produced and directed an American short horror film Guest House. He has also produced and directed two Indian television episodes entitled Trishanku, which were bought by Sony in India. Vince wrote and directed Indian short stories for Indian television channels.

He also directed an American television movie called Sonny. Vince has been producing and directing the Indian television show India Spotlight for the last 20 years. India Spotlight is currently aired in Sacramento, CA.

He is extremely resourceful and creative. Vince is committed to making entertaining and meaningful movies that people want to watch... more than once. He has proven his ability to produce and direct stories with strong characterization and compelling performances.

A Thrilling New Feature Film

Released from prison, Joey becomes an undercover operator for the FBI, tasked with infiltrating the cartel and uncovering the source of the drug. As Joey navigates a dangerous world, he must confront his personal failures and addiction issues. The film builds up to a dramatic conclusion, leaving viewers guessing if Joey can save the day and who is behind the impending attack
